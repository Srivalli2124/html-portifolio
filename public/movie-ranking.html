The Best Movies According To Sri Valli

My top 3 movies of all-time

We Live In Time

Just loved the storyline and the way the actors played out their characters.

My favorite line of the movie :

“I can't bear the thought of being forgotten.”

Tamasha

A good storyline with amazing songs.

My favorite line of the movie :

“Andar se kuch aur hi hain hum. Aur bahar se – majboor.”

Sita Ramam

A deeply emotional tale of love and longing.

My favorite line of the movie :

“To the queen who left behind everything for a soldier with 600 rupees monthly pay. Princess Noorjahan.”
